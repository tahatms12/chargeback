# App Store Listing — CustomsReady Lite

**App name:** CustomsReady Lite
**Tagline:** Generate customs documents for international Shopify shipments

**Description:**
CustomsReady Lite automates the generation of international shipping documents directly from your Shopify admin. Create commercial invoices, CN22, and CN23 forms instantly using your existing order data, ensuring smooth border transitions for your shipments.

Designed for merchants shipping internationally, this tool saves hours of manual data entry by automatically pulling line items, shipping addresses, and declared values into compliant PDF documents ready for print.

Key features include instant HS code lookup, duty and tax estimates, and single-click document export. Ensure your international shipments clear customs without costly delays.
